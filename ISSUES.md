# 🛠️ Issues & Support – BAIS

If you encounter any issues while using **Bubbles Accessibility Input System (BAIS)**, we’re here to help.

---

## 📢 How To Get Help

For the fastest support, please join our Discord community and reach out to staff:

👉 https://fnbubbles420.org/discordinvite

Once inside:

- go to the appropriate support channel
- clearly explain the issue you are experiencing
- a staff member will assist you as soon as possible

---

## 🧾 What To Include

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please provide:

- BAIS version (example: `0.0.20`)
- Operating system (Windows version)
- Input device used (controller, keyboard, mouse, accessibility device, etc.)
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead (error, bug, unexpected behavior)
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- BAIS is a **visible, accessibility-focused tool**
- It does **not** use hidden processes or bypass any protections
- Issues related to unsupported or modified environments may not be reproducible
